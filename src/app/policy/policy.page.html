Privacy Policy

The National Pension and Provident Fund (NPPF) collects and processes personal data such as names, national IDs, contact information, job details, and family information to meet legal, contractual, and service-related obligations. Data is gathered through online applications, customer interactions, and third-party sources and is used strictly for purposes like service delivery, verification, and customer support. NPPF does not share or sell data without user consent unless legally required. Users have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, or object to data processing, as well as request data portability, and all such requests are handled securely within 30 days.

To ensure privacy, NPPF employs strict security measures, including encryption, restricted access, anti-malware tools, and firewalls, in compliance with ISO 27001 and ISO 9001 standards. While cookies are used to enhance user experience and monitor site performance, users can control their cookie preferences. Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary, following strict retention policies. For any complaints, privacy concerns, or data breach incidents, users can contact the appointed Data Protection Officer, who ensures timely resolution of issues.
