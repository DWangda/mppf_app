About Us

The National Pension and Provident Fund manages retirement benefits for civil servants, corporate employees and the member of the armed forces. Currently, pensioners are not required to verify their identity to prove they are alive, which poses substantial risk of income leakage. Verification of pensioners lives and income leakage in pension systems is a significant issue, leading to financial losses and undermining the integrity of pension schemes. Ensuring that pension disbursements reach only the legitimate beneficiaries is crucial.
